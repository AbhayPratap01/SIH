Coal Mining Overview

Coal mining is the process of extracting coal from the ground. Coal is valued for its energy content and has been widely used since the Industrial Revolution. However, coal mining has adverse environmental effects, such as land degradation, water pollution, and air pollution due to dust and emissions.

Prevention & Safety

There are several strategies to minimize the environmental impact of coal mining. Reforestation, water management, and dust control methods are crucial. In addition, mining companies are encouraged to use modern techniques to minimize the release of pollutants and reduce land disruption.
